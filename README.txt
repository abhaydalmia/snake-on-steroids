The game is called SNAKE ON STEROIDS.

In this game, you basially control the snake with the 4 arrow keys.

There are three types of obstacles to avoid.
	1. The green moving bars. The gap position in the bars and the direction
	from which the bar can come, are both random.
	2. Blue Enemy boxes. These boxes can spawn in any of the 4 quadrants
	randomly and will move diagonally from spawn position.
	3. Screen boundaries. Cannot make snake go out of boundary.

COLLISION WITH ANY OF THESE OBSTACLES ARE GAME OVER!


The aim of this game is to avoid the aforementioned obtacles while
COLLECTING FOOD.

Food is the green/blue/red/yellow square and each time you collect
one you get one point.

Only the fittest will survive!
